About Me

Wesley K Clements is a 23-year-old gym assistant with an unfortunate habit of bumping off the people around him. He is witty and funny, but can also be very anxious and a bit humble.

His first victim was his late girlfriend, Heather Fleur Bell, who he killed because she told a joke he didn't particularly appreciate. He has since killed two more people: his brother Connor, and his brother Jeanne.

He is an American who defines himself as straight. He started studying sports science at college but never finished the course. He has phobias of feet and crocodiles

Physically, Wesley is in pretty good shape. He is very tall with fair skin, copper hair and brown eyes. He has a scratch on his cheek gained from a tustle with Connor Trinity Clements just before killing him.

He grew up in a middle class neighbourhood. His father left when he was young, leaving him with his mother, who was an addict.

He is currently single. His most recent romance was with an artist called Madelaine Rory Bailey, who was the same age as him. They broke up because Madelaine wanted somebody more 'badass'.

Wesley's best friend is a gym assistant called Elsa Driscoll. They get on well most of the time. He also hangs around with Kathryn Harrison and Keiran Davies. They enjoy golf together.
